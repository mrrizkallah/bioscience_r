YUM-101 Delicious Foods


Welcome

This is the course website for YUM-101 Delicious Foods.


Course Overview

This 10-week course will give students a foundation in the principles and practice of delicious food appreciation. There will be a special focus on pizzas and pastries. Lectures will be weekly, and lab sections will occur every two weeks, providing students with the opportunity to apply lecture material and assigned readings with hands-on experiences.


Instructors

Rodolphe Lindt

Textbook and Assigned Readings

The textbooks below are not required but will be referred to throughout the course. A copy of each text is being held on reserve at the university library for hourly rental.

On Cooking Authors: Alan M. Hause, Priscilla Martel, and Sarah R. Labensky

On Baking Authors: Eddy Van Damme, Priscilla Martel, and Sarah R. Labensky

When there are assigned articles or readings, these will be posted 2-3 days before the corresponding lecture under the Lectures tab of this course website.
